Why PranCare ?

Are you or your loved one experiencing stress, depression, anxiety, or lack of living up to your full potential? We understand that this can lead to relationship problems, even self sabotaging you. Everything comes with a solution and sometimes you need a helping hand to realise that. PRAN CAREs counsellors will help you and your family members through a variety of proven guidance programmes for psychological, emotional, or behavioural problems. Pran Care services are mainly at three different levels.

Behavioural health for all
This programme aims at providing quality psychological care for all above their social status
E-evaluation for students
This is an app based programme for students to assess their learning ability, IQ, EQ, behavioural evaluation, stress management.
Corporate Guidance
This concept is for employees who find difficulties in their field without realising that self actualisation can do wonders.
