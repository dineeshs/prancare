Assessments


Pran Care offers a wide range of diagnostic and psychometric assessments for both children and adults. Examples of areas where we regularly complete assessment and testing include:

1. Autism Spectrum Disorder (which includes the condition formerly known as Asperger Syndrome) 2. Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) 3. Behavioural Assessment 4. Cognitive Assessment 5. Conduct Disorder 6. Developmental Delay (GDD) 7. Giftedness 8. Intellectual Disability 9. Learning Disorders including Dyslexia, Dyscalculia and Dysgraphia

Please contact us to find out more details about specific assessment tools that we use at Pran Care
